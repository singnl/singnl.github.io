AMP Front Fairing

Carbon fiber fairing with custom aluminum uprights for an autonomous go-kart

CompositesCNC MachiningIn Progress

Designed the front carbon fiber fairing for an autonomous go-kart, including custom aluminum uprights and mounts. Currently in manufacturing.

Foam molds were CNC machined to the outer mold line geometry, and the fairing is being produced via carbon fiber wet layup over those forms. Custom aluminum uprights were designed to interface the fairing structure with the chassis and suspension pickup points.
