PSR Pushrod Suspension

Redesigned front suspension for Purdue Solar Racing — 20% weight reduction

Siemens NXNASTRAN FEACNC MachiningSuspension Kinematics

Redesigned the front suspension architecture for Purdue Solar Racing from a double wishbone configuration to a pushrod system, achieving a 20% weight reduction while improving packaging and load paths into the carbon fiber monocoque.

Suspension geometry was modeled to optimize camber and toe change across the full travel range. Turning, bump, and braking load cases were simulated using FEA in Siemens NASTRAN to validate structural integrity of all components.

All suspension components were designed in Siemens NX from the optimized geometry and fabricated on 3-axis and 5-axis CNC machines.
